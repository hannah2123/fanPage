ICE CREAM

By Hannah K.

Ice cream is a delicious cold treat that had been enjoyed for centuries. Ice cream comes in many different flavors, such as: chocolate, butter pecan, strawberry, rocky road, cookie dough, vanilla, mint chocolate chip, tutti frutti, blue moon, coffee, cookies'n'cream and much more. Ice cream is a great treat to enjoy on a hot day. Ice cream was first invented on 618-97AD in eastern China. It was not until 1904 that people thought to put ice cream in a cone. Ice cream is a delicious treat that is very yummy. If you haven't already, you should, because it will be an experiance you will never forget.

Reasons Why I Like ICE CREAM

  1. It cools you off on a warm day.
  2. It tastes good.
  3. It is kind of healthy

The thing about cones

People have been putting ice cream in cones for a little over a century. Some people like cones, and some would rather have their ice cream in a bowl. There are many types of cones, the most popular are sugar cones and waffle cones. These cones are edible, so you can eat the whole treat. I personally prefer eating my ice cream in a cone. I like being able to eat the whole treat, and the combination of the crunchy cone and the soft ice cream is super tasty.
